APPLYING TO MEDICAL SCHOOLS

The process of applying to medical schools is opaque, costly, and stressful. While I was studying for the MCAT, I wrote a set of notes that eventually became a short general guide for MCAT self-studying. In the interest of transparency, I documented all my costs, travels, and application results in a spreadsheet. I hope that these resources may be useful for anticipating the costs of applying to medical school. Feel free to share.
