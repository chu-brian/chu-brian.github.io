ABOUT ME

I am a medical student at the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania, currently applying for dermatology residency programs. I was a college student at Stanford, where I studied chemistry and computer science. I grew up in San Francisco and attended Lowell High School. I also worked with the business developement team to research competitors across the various vertical markets Doximity operates in.
